  • About the team

    Scherly's reason for joining: Many species have gone extinct, and some are close to being extinct. What Defenders of Wildlife does is not only provide awareness but also work to restore the health and wildlife of vulnerable species and environments. I adore and care about elephants. Defenders of Wildlife also study and advocate for stronger monitoring and law enforcement of trafficked wildlife and wildlife products (I.e., illegal trade of ivory from Elephant tusks). Fasa's reason for joining: Defenders of Wildlife has a great mission. The diversity of the planet is amazing and the wildlife plays a huge part in that diversity. Many species are on the brink of extinction due to human activities. It should be our duty to defend them.
